# LinkedIn Coach- Chrome Extension

This Chrome extension supercharges your LinkedIn experience with AI-powered features designed to boost engagement, networking, and overall professional growth.

## Features

* **AI-Generated Comment Suggestions:**
    * Leverages AI to analyze LinkedIn posts and provide relevant, engaging comment suggestions, saving you time and increasing interaction.
* **Personalized Onboarding & Daily Tasks:**
    * Starts with an onboarding survey to understand your current LinkedIn experience and professional goals.
    * Generates personalized daily tasks with contextual tips to help you achieve your objectives.
* **Smart Profile Suggestions:**
    * Recommends relevant profiles to connect with based on your interests, industry, and goals, expanding your network strategically.
* **Performance Insights & KPIs:**
    * Provides a dashboard with key performance indicators (KPIs) to track your progress:
        * New Followers
        * New Connections
        * New Posts
        * New Comments
    * Allows you to visualize your growth and understand the impact of your LinkedIn activity.

## Installation

1.  **Download the repository:**
    * Click the "Code" button and select "Download ZIP".
    * Extract the downloaded ZIP file to a local directory.
2.  **Enable Developer Mode in Chrome:**
    * Open Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions`.
    * Toggle the "Developer mode" switch in the top right corner.
3.  **Load the unpacked extension:**
    * Click the "Load unpacked" button.
    * Select the directory where you extracted the extension files.
4.  **Pin the extension:**
    * Click the puzzle icon on the top right of your chrome browser, and pin the extension for easy access.
5.  **Open LinkedIn and start using the extension!**

## Usage

1.  **Onboarding:**
    * Upon installation, you will be prompted to complete an onboarding survey.
2.  **AI Comment Suggestions:**
    * When viewing a LinkedIn post, the extension will provide comment suggestions below the post.
3.  **Daily Tasks & Tips:**
    * Access your daily tasks and contextual tips through the extension's popup.
4.  **Profile Suggestions:**
    * Profile suggestions will be shown in the extension's popup or within the linkedin user interface, depending on the implementation.
5.  **Insights Dashboard:**
    * Access your performance insights and KPIs through the extension's popup.
